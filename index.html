About

Kingdom Of Priests Vision

To encourage Priests to be involve in Social Economic Development of local communities. To have Community centres in local communities where Priests resolve community matters. To act as a community think tank where Priests lead Community strategy planning To promote Communual/ Collective Ownership of local Economy.

Kingdom Of Priests mission

To creat new Priesthood that will be vanguard of local community. to use unelected structure of the community to be true drivig force of unity, Ubuntu and socio ecomic development of local communities. To control local economy and rebuild the African communial ownership of means of production. by all the above, we are on realizing the godly vision of Kingdom Of Priests.
